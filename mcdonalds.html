McDonalds Interface Evaluation

In this project I evaluated the UX factors on the McDonalds store Interfaces.

The McDonald's kiosk/shop interface are stationed in McDonalds shops as a quick and simple way to place a self-service order. The devices are touch-screen and use images to show the products that the store offers.

The Interface

Context

People generally use those devices to avoid queues or to place an order themselves. The interface permits the user to navigate through all the available items and gives the possibility to modify your order..

User

THe public that make use of those devices are people that consumes McDonalds in store or to take away.

Conclusion

To conclude, the overall experience is simple and logical giving the user autonomy to place their own order. The user can understand quickly how to operate it and follow all the steps from begining to end. The interface could get some improvement in their aesthetics with a better design and better visual stimulation. It could as well offer guidance for first time users and possibly trigger to buy other products or get offers while using the device.
