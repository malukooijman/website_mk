I am currently working on my UI/UX design skills at The Hague University of Applied Science. About me

I am currently based in Rotterdam, NL.

I'm a 90's Millennial that loves exploring new places and learning new things. I have a passion for art, design and architecture, while I can admire something for hours and imagine possible improvements for it. I enjoy to explore how something was made/build or understand what was the original purpose of that item/place. Alongside, I enjoy understanding people and their behaviour. My degree in Psychology helped me to master it, especially, how to handle and interpret people in an empathic way.

I've lived in Brazil, England and I'm currently placed in the Netherlands. I enjoy travelling so I won't reject any abroad opportunities to improve or learn skills while offering my greatest work results.
